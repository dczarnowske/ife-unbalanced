"Inference in Unbalanced Panel Data Models with Interactive Fixed Effects" by Daniel Czarnowske and Amrei Stammann - Replication Package

R-Files:

1_1_figure.R - generates Figure 1.
1_2_simulation1.R - conducts the monte carlo study described in Section 4.
1_3_simulation1_results.R - generates Tables 1 and 2.
1_4_empirical_example.R - generates Figures 2 and 3, and Tables 3 and 4. Requires the data set 'DDCGdata_final.dta' from the replication package of Acemoglu et al. (2019), https://www.journals.uchicago.edu/doi/suppl/10.1086/700936
2_1_matrix_completion_comparison.R - conducts the small monte carlo study described in Section A.2 (appendix).
2_2_matrix_completion_comparison_results.R - generates Tables 5 and 6 (appendix).
2_3_empirical_example.R - Figures 4 and 5 (appendix). Requires the data set 'DDCGdata_final.dta' from the replication package of Acemoglu et al. (2019), https://www.journals.uchicago.edu/doi/suppl/10.1086/700936
3_1_simulation1_results.R - generates Tables 7 and 8 (online supplement).
3_2_figure.R - generates Figure 6 (online supplement).
3_3_simulation2.R - conducts the monte carlo study described in Section B.2 (online supplement).
3_4_simulation2_results.R - generates Tables 9-14 (online supplement).

Folders:

figures/ - contains Figures 1-6 as pdf files.
tables/ - contains Tables 1-14 as txt files
